about us

La Mode established itself as a trusted source of unique ladies footwear as we proudly announce our products as “Made in Bangladesh”. We are based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. La Mode is renowned for its fashion authority, unique design & craftsmanship with the best quality. La Mode dedicates itself to quality, unique design, and exceptional value for the price. Come join the spirit of ultimate fashion footwear to indulge yourself with uniqueness & sophistication which is constructed with high-quality craftsmanship.

We welcome you to the world of La Mode.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make La Mode a household name by keeping our commitment to producing high- quality items from sustainable, ethically sourced materials and by providing superior service to everyone. Our Mission outlines:
  • Making La Mode a household name
  • Producing high-quality handcrafted items
  • Providing superior service
  • Keeping commitment to quality

Our Vision

To establish La Mode as an internationally recognized and trusted brand for ethically produced handcrafted products. Our Vision outlines:
  • Bringing La mode to the world stage
  • Gaining international recognition for Bangladesh’s handcrafted shoe-making industry
  • Bringing La mode to all parts of Bangladesh
